Hands-on Modeling & Simulation

From Data to Model: Using medical imaging, 3D scans, or sensor data like pressure measurements, I build precise models that suit your project’s requirements.

Flexible Output Formats: I deliver models as CAD files, surface meshes, FEM meshes, or lattice structures designed from biomechanical input.

Contact Modeling Expertise: I'm experienced in modeling contact between adjacent bodies, with aligned interface FEM nodes for optimized simulations.

Mesh: I do homogeneous tetrahedral meshes. If you provide me with a more complex, structured FEM mesh, I can deform it into a patient-specific geometry.

Focus on Modeling: I prefer to handle the modeling process, leaving simulation parameter definition to your specifications.

Strategic Consulting & Guidance

Data-to-Model Expertise: I support you in evaluating how you get to your individualized medical product accurately and effectively.

Numerical Simulations Strategy: I provide strategic guidance for selecting appropriate simulation methods for your goals.

Biomechanical Product Design: Working with you to optimize workflows, adapt modeling & simulation strategies, and integrate biomechanical insights effectively into your product design.
